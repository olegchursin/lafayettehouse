Welcome to Lafayette House

This iconic brown stone was built in 1848, as Lafayette Street emerged as one of the most fashionable streets in New York City. Lovingly restored in 2006, Lafayette House offers marble fire places, deep soak bathtubs and lush outdoor spaces. Whether you are a native New Yorker looking for an escape or a traveler in search of a serene refuge, welcome back to Old New York.
